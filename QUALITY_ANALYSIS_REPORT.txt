5. WEAK/VAGUE TUTOR MODE HINTS
================================================================================
COUNT: Approximately 59+ questions with weak "Tänk på" hints (14% of hints)
SEVERITY: MEDIUM - Hints are questions, not guidance

PATTERN: "Tänk på..." (Think about...)
These are prompts, not progressive hints. Examples:
  - trauma-001: "Tänk på ABCDE-akronymen - vad betyder A?" (prompts thinking, doesn't teach)
  - hoeft-001: "Tänk på vilken artär som går medialt om höften" (question, not hint)
  - hoeft-003: "Tänk på biomekaniken: var går krafterna vid en subtrochantär fraktur?"
  - hoeft-013: "Tänk på hur hävarm påverkar muskelfunktion - längre hävarm = mer kraft"
  - hoeft-019: "Tänk på "taper-slip" - stammen sjunker gradvis tills jämvikt uppnås"

QUALITY ASSESSMENT:
Non-progressive hints: ~52% of hint blocks analyzed
  - Hints don't increase in specificity/detail
  - Hints are similar in complexity
  - Fails to provide progressive scaffolding

WEAK HINT STRUCTURE ISSUES:
  - Not clinically actionable: "Vilken", "Vad är", "Jämför"
  - Too vague: "Skillnaden mellan..."
  - Questions masked as hints: "Think about X" rather than "X is important because..."

EXAMPLE OF WEAK PROGRESSION:
  Hint 1: "Tänk på ABCDE-akronymen"
  Hint 2: "Vad är det allra första som krävs för att överleva?"
  Hint 3: "Airway (luftväg) kommer alltid först i ATLS-protokollet"
  
  ISSUE: Hint 1 and 2 are questions, not progressive hints. Better would be:
  Hint 1: "Remember ABCDE - what does A stand for?"
  Hint 2: "Without airway, patients cannot survive - why is this first?"
  Hint 3: "ATLS prioritizes A (Airway) before B (Breathing) and C (Circulation)"
